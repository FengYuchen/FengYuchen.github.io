About

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego. I have worked with Prof. Truong Q. Nguyen at Video Processing Lab since Fall 2013. I was a research intern at Honda Research Institute during summer 2016, woring with Dr. Ashish Tawari. I was an immersive imaging research intern at Hewlett-Packard Company during summer and winter 2015, working with Dr. Kar-Han Tan and Dr. Daniel Tretter. I received the M.S. degree from UC San Diego in 2015 and the B.S. degree from Yonsei University, Republic of Korea in 2013. My research interests are computer vision, human-machine interaction, and machine learning.
